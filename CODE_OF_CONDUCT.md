# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free, and inclusive community.

Examples of expected behavior:

- constructive technical discussion
- respectful disagreement
- accepting feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or discriminatory language
- personal attacks
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that violate these standards.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainers.
